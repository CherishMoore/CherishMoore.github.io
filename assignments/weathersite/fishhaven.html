Hometwon Pride At It's Fullest

Rosie Loore proudly stands in front of her family farm after winning blue ribbons at the county fair for her corn entry. She has spent her summer working hard with her father to plant, care for, and harvest a a beautiful crop of corn this season.
"I like getting my hands dirty and working hard" she says. "It makes me feel good when I see the seeds start to sprout and know that I am on my way to harvesting something that will help other people."
Rosie is an 8th grader and has big plans to raise some pigs next year. She wants to enter them in the fair also. When she's not working on the farm she enjoys playing soccer and loves listening to music.
